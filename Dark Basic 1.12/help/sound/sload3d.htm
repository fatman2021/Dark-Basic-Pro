LOAD 3DSOUND
This command will load a WAV sound file into the specified Sound Number as a special 3D sound. 3D sounds can be placed in 3D space and heared through the virtual ears of a listener. The listener can also be placed anywhere in 3D space creating true surround sound capabilities. The Sound Number must be an integer value.

SYNTAX
LOAD 3DSOUND Filename, Sound Number
